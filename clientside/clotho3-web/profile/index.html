Welcome to Phagebook.
A more holistic approach to synthetic biology research.

Phagebook is a Clotho 3.0 app that serves as a social networking interface incorporating lab inventory management and project/personnel networking.

Connecting researchers to a social networking platform that encourages sharing, data exchange, standardization, and collaboration.

Phagebook facilitates collaboration amongst the synthetic biology community and like any other Clotho app, it directly communicates withClotho apps for specification, design, assembly, and verification of synthetic biological systems.
